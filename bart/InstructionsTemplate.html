In this task, you must decide how many air pumps you send into a balloon. Initially, the balloon is deflated. Each pump earns you {{ C.PAYOFF_PER_PUMP|cu }}, so the more the balloon is inflated, the more money you earn, unless the balloon explodes, in which case you lose everything.

The balloon can withstand at most {{ C.MAX_PUMPS }} pumps, but it can explode at any pump: 1st, 2nd, ..., {{ C.MAX_PUMPS }}th.
Concretely, the computer program randomly picks a number between 1 and {{ C.MAX_PUMPS }}; this number determines the pump at which the balloon explodes. Each number has the same probability of being drawn.

To set the number of air pumps you want to send into the balloon, use the buttons and . You can click the buttons or keep them pressed.

Once your decision is made, click the Validate button. You will then see whether the balloon explodes or not. If the balloon explodes, your payoff is {{ 0|cu }}; otherwise, you earn {{ C.PAYOFF_PER_PUMP |cu }} per pump.
