You are part of a pair randomly formed by the computer program. You cannot identify the other player in your pair, and they cannot identify you.

For each of the {{ C.matrices|length }} matrices below, you must choose an allocation of money between yourself and the other player in your pair. Amounts are expressed in experimental currency (ECU). The conversion rate from ECU to Euros is 1 ECU = {{ C.conversion_rate|c }}.

In each matrix, the top row indicates the amount for you, and the bottom row indicates the amount for the other player in your pair. To choose an allocation, click on the column corresponding to your choice. You must choose one allocation in each matrix before you can validate the page.

To determine each player's payoff in the pair, the computer program will first randomly select one of the {{ C.matrices|length }} matrices. It will then randomly determine whether your allocation or the other player's allocation is applied in your pair. If your allocation is applied, then for the selected matrix your allocation determines both payoffs: your payoff equals the amount in the "For you" row, and the other player's payoff equals the amount in the "For the other player" row. If the other player's allocation is applied, then for the selected matrix your payoff equals the amount in the "For the other player" row, and their payoff equals the amount in the "For you" row.
