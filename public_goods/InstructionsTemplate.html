General framework

At the start of the experiment, the computer program will randomly form groups of {{ subsession.players_per_group }} people. The composition of these groups will remain unchanged throughout the experiment. You cannot identify the other members of your group, and they cannot identify you.

The experiment consists of {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} identical periods. At the beginning of each period, you receive {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} tokens that you must fully allocate between two accounts: your private account and a public account shared by your group.

How the accounts work

Private account

Each token you place in your private account gives you 1 ECU (for you only). Likewise, if another member places a token in their private account, it gives them 1 ECU (for them only).

Example: If you place 5 tokens in your private account, your payoff from this account will be 5 ECU.
Public account

The public account is shared by the {{ subsession.players_per_group }} members of the group. Each token placed in the public account gives {{ subsession.mpcr }} ECU to each group member.

Example: {{ instructions_exemple_compte_collectif }}

Payoff calculation

Your payoff for the period is the sum of your payoff from the private account and your payoff from the public account.

Example: {{ instructions_exemple_gain }}
{{ if subsession.seuil > 0 }}
⚠️ Minimum threshold warning

If the total number of tokens placed by your group in the public account does not reach the minimum threshold of {{ subsession.seuil }} tokens, then each group member's payoff from the public account is 0 ECU. In that case, each player's payoff for the period is limited to the number of tokens placed in their own private account.

{{ endif }}

Sequence of a period

On screen, you only enter the number of tokens placed in the public account. The remaining tokens are automatically assigned to your private account.

Decisions are made simultaneously. Once everyone in the group has submitted, a summary screen appears showing: your allocations, the group total placed in the public account, and your payoff for the period.

You will always have access to a tabular history showing the results of past periods.

{{ if subsession.chat }}

At the beginning of each period, you can chat for {{ chat_time }} with the other members of your group through a chat interface.

{{ endif }}
Your payoff for the experiment equals your cumulative payoff over the {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} periods.
